Eine 3D Druckerei in der 3-ten Welt

by Svetlin Ivanov

In order to find out how technology is improving development in Indonesia, Sventlin gains insight into the work taking place at a 3D-printing factory in Jakarta. There he speaks to Wadi Chan, a 30-year enterpeneur and CEO of the printing company which produce a range of items, from lampshades, to helmets, to components for Aerospace engineering projects.
